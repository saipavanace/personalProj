GPRAR.Policy

GPRAR.ReadID

GPRAR.WriteID

Policy determines the setting of our internal OR[1:0] field which defines
the behavior of the agent at the bottom of Ncore

ReadID=1 implies NoOrdering by ArID ie free listing for reads.

WriteID=1 implies NoOrdering by AwID ie free listing for writes.

Only supported combinations:

  1. Policy = 2’b11, WriteID = 1’b0, ReadID = 1’b0

  2. Policy = 2’b10, WriteID = 1’b1, ReadID = 1’b1

  3. Policy = 2’b10, WriteID = 1’b0, ReadID = 1’b0

Ncore 3.7 System Architecture Specification version 1.0.9
