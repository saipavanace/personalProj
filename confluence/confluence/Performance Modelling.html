For v1.5, we will bring up our performance modeling support as follows.  


Phase 1. Verilated Coherent Subsystem with Platform Architect Support.

 

 

Notes:

  • The pin-to-FT-AXI and pin-to-FT-ACE bridges are those provided by SNPS as part of Platform Architect.
  • A Platform Architect script is used to create a PA subsystem including the verilated coherent subsystem and the pin-to-FT-XI and pin-to-FT-ACE adapters.
  • The CSR initializer is implemented in verilog and verilated with the rest of the coherent subsystem.  After reset, it reads information about the various IP blocks from the CSR space, and then writes to the various blocks to enable snoop filters and other functionality.
  • The user may connect this to one or more NCTI(s) AV or VV models in Platform architect if they want.
