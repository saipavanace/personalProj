Optimization Opportunities

  • Perhaps size the HTT data less than HTT-Ctrl, taking advantage of the latency of the embedded memory.  
  • The control portion of the output of FIFO 15 feeds into 2 arms of the 3-input DTR output mux.  We could optimize by using a 2:1 mux for the control portion.
  • The output of the RTT & HTT data structures have error detect and correct serially after the memory read.  This could be optimized by putting the correct logic off to the side with a pipeline bubble.
  • HTT & RTT memories could be a single memory.
