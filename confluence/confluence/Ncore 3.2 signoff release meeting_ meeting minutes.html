Design/ RTL:

hw_config_10_lite

The wort violated paths are related to the proxy-cache in ioaiu_top_g block.

96 OTTs is used because of the large timing violations with 128 OTTs @ 1.6GHz.

Projection is that the max frequency is limited to 1.2GHz instead of 1.6GHz with the proxy cache and/ or large OTT values.
