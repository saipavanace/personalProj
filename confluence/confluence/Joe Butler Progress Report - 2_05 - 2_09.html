Executive Summary

Ncore Performance Modeling effort made good progress integrating the C++ checker to expand functional testing and debug.  Basic cases are passing with checker – this is a key step to enabling a Release Candidate this week for Ncore2.5.  The Symphony HW Spec 0.9 was released and initial reviews were held.  Maestro 0.7 SW Spec advanced and will be released / reviewed this coming week.  We are starting to hear of interest in Ncore3.0 (Socionext and Esperanto) and are helping Pre-sales with early feedback.  Need more detailed requirements including schedule to understand how best to meet the customers’ requirements.  January timebox results and February timebox objectives were shared in Engineering All-Hands (see results below).  Symphony Legato RTL coding continues including first-pass RTL for the switch.  Finally, Functional Safety investment continues with Jama training last week and ISO26262 training this week for the rest of engineering.
