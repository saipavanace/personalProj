Summary

Bottom line, Synopsys has no D2D solution with a digital interface that is ready for NXP to use out of the box. Arteris IP or NXP would have to create the digital I/F to the XSR SERDES or HBI interface. HBI is an HBM-derived PHY that most likely required interposer connection between die. XSR is SERDES with fewer wires and lower cost packaging options. Synopsys also supports AIB and CCIX integration with Arm CMN-600 interconnect.

Overall purpose of call was to find a lower latency I/F than CCIX that we could use for Ncore 3-to-Ncore 3 connection in NXP's EAP design so we could offer better and lower risk option than NXP's proposed CMN-600 based proposal.
