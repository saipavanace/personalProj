There are three (3) different "conformance levels" of the protocol:

    1. TileLink Uncached Lightweight (TL-UL) - Chapter 6
    2. TileLink Uncached Heavyweight (TL-UH) - Chapter 7

    3. TileLink Cached (TL-C) - Chapter 8

WD OmniXtend Specification

This WD OmniXtend Specification describes how the TileLink protocol can be serialized for transmission over chip-to-chip interconnects, and should be read in conjunction with the TileLink specification (currently version 1.7-draft).
