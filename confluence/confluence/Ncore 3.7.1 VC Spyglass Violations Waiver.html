 Root Causes Analysis

Lint Tests 31 Dec 2024

Cause

Effect

Customers use Synopsys VC Spyglass and Cadence Xrun Lint tool.

Cadence Xrun Lint tool was not run: any violation not reported by Spyglass and reported by Xrun was unknown and not planned to be fixed.

Engineering team prioritized the resolution of the critical Lint error because some violations are ultimately waivable.

Report shared with NXP with the statement that the violations reported by the Lint run were waivable.

Low priority Lint violations are going to be resolved as part of the product continuous improvement process.

Jira tickets created and progress tracked in Confluence.
