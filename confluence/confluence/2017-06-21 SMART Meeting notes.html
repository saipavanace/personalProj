1hrPower and Clock DomainsJohn Coddington (Deactivated)
  • spec only covers domains that are controllable; does not cover domains that aren't controllable, nor circuitary that is needed to connect domains
  • PMA = power management agent
  • is there a way a master / slave to negotiate with ATU?
    • a lot of times the system SW will do the proper draining of signals
    • OCP-IP has a way to do power handshake at interface
  • QACTIVE = busy upstream? or connected to request from IP that is connected to ATU
  • all blocks will have busy signal connected to PMA
  • concept of retention: can lower power but won't lose state; will have additional retention reset
  • P channel - defined by ARM for power; Q channel - for clock
    • CCI has both P and Q channel
  • power domain crossing - need to keep track of any pending transactions
    • require adapter
    • need to send an error response
    • need this link to be symmetrical on request and response (sw will need to do this restriction)
  • logic clock gating - can verify with assertion, or in verification testplan
