RTL signoff status:

Lint results:

Low number of Errors (~10) are waivable. The fixes are too risky and not implemented.

List of waivers is provided.

RTL open JIRA tickets:

No open RTL bug while one issue is opened to update the architecture specification to match the RTL behavior which is correct CONC-11848 .

Timing analysis:

DC-NXT run completed for the HW-Config10-Lite and the signoff checklist has been updates with the results. 3 large timing violations have been discussed.

  • chi_async_adapter_a: violation is due to missing constraint in Maestro script (false paths between the 2 clock domains). Issue has been solved MAES-6055 .

  • DMI: DC-NXT is not able to fully optimize the unit. Synthesis and STA shall be run with RTLA and optimized floorplan to check realistically the timing

  • IOAIU_TOP_G is a XL block and timing results are not accurate. Synthesis and STA shall be run with RTLA to check realistically the timing: DCNXT couldn’t complete optimization even after running for 2 days.

Timing report from Fusion Compiler/ RTLA will be reported instead of DC-NXT starting with Ncore 3.6 onward.

AI: re-run the synthesis on chi_async_adapter_a block to confirm the violations vanished => Own = Eric

AI: re-run the synthesis on IOAIU_TOP_G unit to confirm the small violations remain after optimization is complete => Own = Eric
