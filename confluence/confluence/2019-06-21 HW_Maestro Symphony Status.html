Design Progress

  • Updating CPRs as needed by SW.
  • Completed CTL Queue logic.
  • Started coding rate limiter logic.
  • Held reviews of the CTL interface.
    • Going to reorganized interface to match SMI example. 
  • Held a discussion about the various forms of protection
    • sb_prot and sb_prot_b are side band protection signals added to any interface at the physical layer.
      • The old names prot and prot_b overlapped with AXI names and so needed to change.
    • ib_prot is an in band protection signal added to an interface that ultimately lands in the packet and not on ATP signals. This is passed through and ignore by Symphony logic.
    • All forms of protection in a native protocol will by mapped to fields in the packet or terminated at the ATU-I blocks and regenerated at the ATU-T blocks.
