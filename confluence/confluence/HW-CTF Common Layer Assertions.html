Common Queue

The common queue will have an assert per in channel, queue channel, and out channel. The assert will only be instanced if there is more than 1 or said channel.

ASSERT_COMMONQ_IN_\=in=\_HIT 

ASSERT_COMMONQ_QUEUE_\=queue=\_HIT

ASSERT_COMMONQ_QUEUE_\=out=\_HIT
