1. Does sys_event_receiver aggregate events that it is receiving from multiple Aius?

    1. Yes, the sys_evt_receiver block aggregates all the sysReq messages that are received within a certain time period (all the sysReq messages are received while sys_evt_receiver state_machine is neither in ST_IDLE nor ST_DONE are aggregated to one) and only generates one event_out_req. When it gets the response, it responds to all the Aius.
