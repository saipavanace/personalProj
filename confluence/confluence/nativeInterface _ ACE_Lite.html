Overview

The ACE-Lite interface is a defined subset of the full ACE interface. ACE-Lite is used by master components that do no have hardware coherent caches, but are required to:

  • indicate if issued transactions could be held in the hardware coherent caches of other masters

  • issue barrier transactions. (Ncore does not support it)

  • issue broadcast cache maintenance operations.

axaddr

addr will be covered in the following distinct bins-

  1. cache-aligned (ex. for 64B cacheline-size, addr[5:0]=0)

  2. cache-unaligned && burst-size aligned

(ex. for 64B cacheline and 16B bus-size, addr[5:4]!=0 && addr[3:0]==0

  1. cache-unaligned && burst-size unaligned

(ex. for 64B cacheline and 16B bus-size, addr[5:4]!=0 and addr[3:0]!=0)

coherent addr

axcache

0 - Bufferable

2 - Read Allocate
