Install NoMachine client from here:https://www.nomachine.com/download as per your OS and finish the setup with default options

1. Click “New” or "Add"

2. Protocol “NX”.

3. Click “Continue”

4. Type “Host = cline.arteris.com” (keep the rest default – as is)

5. Click “Continue”

6. Select “Password” option.

7. Click “Continue”

8. Select “Don’t use a proxy”

9. Click “Continue”

10. Click “Done”

11. Click “Connect” and enter your username and password.

12. Once Connected click “New Desktop”

13. Click “New Virtual Desktop”

14. Your desktop is ready.

15. If you don’t like dual cursors. Goto Input options and un-select “Show remote cursor pointer”
