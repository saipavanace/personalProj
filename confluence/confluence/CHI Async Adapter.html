Description


The CHI Async Adapter has a slave CHI interface and a master CHI interface, each interface has its own clock.

A CHI interface comprises 6 channels of flits, and also sideband signals for SActive and SysCo.

The 6 channels of flits on the CHI interface are:

    RX REQ FLIT
    RX RSP FLIT
    RX DAT FLIT
    TX REQ FLIT
    TX RSP FLIT
    TX DAT FLIT


The block diagram for the CHI Async Adapter is shown as below.

NOTE: The depth of the gen_async_adapter is set to nCHIReqInFlight + 1.
