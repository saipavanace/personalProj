Config10_lite specifications and performance (Typical 0.75v 85C)

Overall Summary:

All blocks meet 1.6GHz** target. The ioaiu with 2MB proxy cache which is currently 1.56GHz** without timing optimizations.

Blocks builds:

  • Synthesis Tool = Design Compiler -topographical

  • TSMC 7nm

  • target freq = 1.6GHz 

  • Typical Corner reported: Typical process, 0.75v, 85C

  • 5% limit on use of ULVT cells

  • 15% clock uncertainty

** These were synthesized with DC_TOPO & available SRAM’s [Doesn’t factor slow SRAMs, PnR, CTS...]

IOAIU Summary: (no resiliency, QosEnabled)

  • Instance A-F meet the target frequency of 1.6GHz

  • The “g” instance has a 2MB proxy cache and is slightly below target however, I believe with some floorplanning improvements it can reach 1.6 GHz at typical corner as well
