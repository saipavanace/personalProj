Coverage Analysis

  • In Questa, We mainly focus on Statements Coverage (Synopsys dve equivalent is 'line coverage') and FEC condition & expression coverage (Synopsys merges both of them to 'Contional coverage').
  • Attached Email discussion from Mentor AE on explanation about FEC condition & expression coverage.

 

Email Conversion with Mentor AE on FEC coverage

 

 Condition coverage — analyzes the decision made in “if” and ternary statements and can be considered as an extension to branch coverage.

 

Expression coverage — analyzes the expressions on the right hand side of assignment statements, and is similar to condition coverage.

 

Our understanding is VCS doesn’t have expr coverage. They ‘mostly’ cover logical expressions anywhere in RTL as condition coverage. It is often subset of our combined expression and condition coverage.

 

But, here is a simple one to illustrate:

 

assign xx = (xyz == abc) ? a1 : a2;

 

We will do branch and cond coverage on it. VCS will do branch and condition coverage on it.

 

 Initial a = xyz && abc;

 

 We will do expression coverage on it. VCS will do condition coverage on it.
