Overview

When snoop filter ends up tracking a cacheline as only sharer and no owners. Sharer promotion allows to promote one of the sharers as an owner for snooping and data retrieval purposes

Remarks

Random Testcases

SnpReq’s UP and MPF3 values are randomized with the Constraints

  • Unique Owner/Sharer → UP == ‘b01 and Mpf3 == Dont’care

  • Multiple Sharers and Owner Identified → UP == 'b11 and MPF3 == Owner FunitId

  • Multiple Sharers and Owner Not Identified → UP == 'b11 MPF3 == UnitFunitID (SnpClnDtr, SnpVldDtr,SnpNoSDInt and SnpNitc) or MPF3 == random AIU FunitId
