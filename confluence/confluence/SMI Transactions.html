cmstatus.Snarf

For Stashing CMDreqs:
Snarf = 1: The target snooper agent has accepted the invitation to receive the stashed data.
Snarf = 0: The target snooper agent has declined the invitation to receive the stashed data.

CMStatus[5:0] = {5:1 Reserved | Snarf}
