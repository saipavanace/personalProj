Ncore3 ArchitectureSanjay Deshpande (Deactivated)
  • we can only support far atomics at DMI (i.e. access going to memory), but not for IO accesses
  • Traffic separation is done by address, rather by semantic (a specific address from a single master will always go to the same destination)
  • Discussion around mixing of coherent/non-coherent traffic in terms of performance
  • There may be inefficiency to route all traffic (coherent and non-coherent) through Ncore. Message to customer is also to use a non-coherent interconnect, rather than message from ARM CCN/CMN, where all traffic is absorbed through the coherent fabric
  • Planned for broadcast messages in transport (for future)
  • For deadlock avoidance, need to either:
    • ensure messages are sent only when there is available credit (could be costly) - this is for first release
    • use VC (for future)
