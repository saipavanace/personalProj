 Root Causes Analysis

The timing violations colored in amber are small (<100ps), they can be closed by optimizing the floorplan and the constraints.

The timing violations colored in red are large (>100ps).
The Macro2Flop timing violations are from Memory2Flop paths and these paths can be met by using multi-cycle wrapper around the memory.
The ones from RTLA run are due to the lack of uLVT.

RTL from similar configuration have been run with Fusion Compiler and the timing can be closed.
