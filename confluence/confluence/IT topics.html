Recovering a deleted folder from /home

Backups of your home directory happen on a daily basis.

Go to /home/<user>/.snapshots. There you will see the available snapshots:

[jcodd@mondavi ~]$ cd /home/jcodd/.snapshots
[jcodd@mondavi ~/.snapshots]$ ls -lt
