Clock Interfaces are composed of multiple signals but must have the following properties:

1) Only one signal can contain the string "clk" and that signal shall be the clock.

2) Only one signal can contain the string "reset_n" and that signal shall be the reset signal and it is active low.

3) There must be at least two signals that meet rules 1 and 2.

Example TACHL code to hook up clock and reset:
