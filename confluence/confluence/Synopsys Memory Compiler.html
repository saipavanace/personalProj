1.Open a new terminal and log into a 64-bit machine such as cline and type in these command lines to launch the Synopsys Memory Compiler called Integrator:


unsetenv SNPSLMD_LICENSE_FILE

setenv SNPSLMD_LICENSE_FILE 5285@lic-node0.arteris.com:5285@lic-node1.arteris.com

mkdir MEM_gen

cd MEM_gen

source /engr/dev/tools/scripts/snps-eng.sh

/engr/dev/tools/synopsys/memory/EmbedIt-Integrator/embedit_integrator_vP-2019.09/bin/integrator &


2. When the Integrator GUI shows up, click on the "Project" pull down menu and then click on the item "New". Then a sub-window with the title "Create Project" appears. Enter the field "Project Name" (for example, test_mem). Then click on the "OK" button.

3. Click on the "Project" pull down menu and then click on the item "Libraries". Then a sub-window with the title "Compiler Libraries" appears. Click on the "Add" button. Then a sub-window with the title "Add Library" appears. Enter the field "Folder Name" (for example, /engr/dev/tools/synopsys/memory/TSMC16nm/install_dir)(for example, /engr/dev/tools/synopsys/memory/TSMC7). Click on the "Add" button. Click on the "OK" button.

4. Click on the "Component" pull down menu and then clock on the item "New". Then a sub-window with the title "Select Compiler Or Type" appears. Double click on the "memory" folder to expand it. Double click on the "TSMC" folder to expand it. Double click on the "16nm GL FF+ P-Optional Vt/Cell Std Vt" folder to expand it. Click on one of the items (for example, SiWare Two Port High Density Leakage Control Register File 128K Sync)(for example, SiWare Dual Port High Density Leakage Control SRAM 1M Sync), and the corresponding text box "Compiler Information" will display the detail information. Underneath the text box are two buttons: "View Documentation", "View Range Table". Click any of them to view the documentation. Otherwise click on the "Configure" button.

5. A sub-window with the title "Configure Component" appears. Enter each of the fields with the appropriate value. For example, "Number of Words": 2K, Bits in Word "40", etc. Click on the "OK" button. Then click on the "Close" button on the sub-window "Select Compiler Or Type" that was opened earlier. 

6. Click on the "Run" pull down menu. Click on the "Target" item. Click on the "Generate" item. You should see some folders with files are generated under your directory "MEM_gen/test_mem". Note that "test_mem" was entered into the "Project Name" in the beginning. You should see this generated file "MEM_gen/test_mem/compout/views/*/ssgnp_*/*.lib". And you need to convert this .lib file to .db file that is to be linked in the actual synthesis run.


7.Exit the Integrator GUI and type this command to launch the Library Compiler.


/engr/dev/tools/synopsys/lc_vP-2019.03-SP5/amd64/syn/bin/lc_shell
