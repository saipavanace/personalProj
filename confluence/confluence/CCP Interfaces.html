Stimulus Description

Since we are looking at interfaces of an internal block within IOAIU, the CCP block. Refer to nativeInterface:AXI4 for Stimulus Description. In general stimulus on nativeInterface:AXI4 should be allocating reads (identified by arcache[2]=1) and allocating writes (identified by awcache[3]=1)
