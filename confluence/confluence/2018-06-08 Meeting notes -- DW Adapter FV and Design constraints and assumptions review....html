Goals

  • To review and list the design assumptions for DW Adapter, as well as to review and list the constraints in the formal verification environment. 

Discussion items

TimeItem Notes
30 minDesign Assumptions Review 
  • NULL_D fields are restricted to lie only on the uppermost bits of a data-beat. In Header-parallel case, NULL_D can exist between Header and Data fields.
  • No restrictions on the order of the valid (Fixed and Clone) fields.
  • Ratio (N) of the data-field widths of the ingress and egress links are always in integral powers of 2. Ex: 1:2, 4:1, 1:8 etc.
  • FIFO depths are assumed to be always set to integral powers of 2 and also 0.
  • Refer the action items for rest of the notes.
30 minFormal Assumptions/Constraints Review 
  • Link Packet Definitions - Without Randomizer tool, the number of configurations of LPDs that can be generated is limited.
  • VC version of DW Adapter - Can be verified in simulation since the wrapper for vc_dw_adapter just involves connecting instances of dw_adapter, vc_demux, and vc_mux.
  • Back-pressure at the master-side of DW Adapter scenario is being covered.
  • Reduced or non-uniform rate scenarios of beat injection into DW Adapter are being covered.
  • Refer the action items for rest of the notes.
