GOALS:

  1. Bring-up first full "Presto" configuration with 16 AXI ATUi (initiators side), 16 AXI ATUt (target side) and two (request & response fabric) bi-directional rings (16x16 each).
  2. We should be able to pass through approximately 1000 random transactions (read/write) per AXI ATUi (initiator side) successfully across and received the expected response.
  3. Top level scoreboard should be able verify the correctness of all of these transactions (read / write) with responses. And also make sure each transaction reach to proper target.
