Architecture Progress

  • Reviewed with designers how to handle packet errors when they are discovered.
    • Well documented in Symphony ArchDef.
  • Held discussions about how to handle internal state errors in network blocks of Symphony.
    • Decision to freeze output valids low and let ATU timers throw error.
    • Avoids having to route lots of error wires to the fault monitor.
    • Should be sufficient for ADAS use cases.
