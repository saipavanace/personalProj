5 Key Areas of Focus:

○Area 1: Native Protocol Assessment

•Understanding Native protocol semantics
•Understanding the requirements of Native protocols and their impact on Symphony architecture
•Defining the Common Transport Layer functionality to support Native Protocol requirements
•Address map translation

○Area 2: Switch design investigation

•Modular switch design investigation given that:
  • Have to be comparable to FlexNoc in area and power for the low end systems
  • Meet the requirements of high end high performance systems

Investigation to understand:

○Modularity
○Area
○Power
○Performance
○Impact on overall Symphony architecture

○Area 3: End-to-End Credit Management

•Feasibility of Credit management scheme
•Buffer sizing
•Protocol development
•Understanding impact on overall architecture

○Area 4: Other HW blocks:

•Symphony has to offer similar HW features as FlexNoc. This task will handle definition of all of those blocks

○Area 5: Routing investigation and Simulation

•To support irregular topologies we would need to come up with a routing scheme. This area will investigate that and come up with recommendations
•Start simulation studies to investigate multiple areas
