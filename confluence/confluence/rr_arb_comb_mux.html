Descriptions

Round-robin arbiter, high speed, zero-cycle, that can be configured to support interleave or non-interleave. Default non-interleave.

When configured to non-interleave mode, the arbiter makes a new decision on the cycle when the last beat is transferred from the sink side to the source side.

When configured to interleave mode, the arbiter makes a new decision every cycle when a beat is transferred from the sink side to the source side.

The arbiter provides a one-hot grant bit vector on the sink side for the external logic to do data multiplexing. The arbiter doesn't do data multiplexing.
