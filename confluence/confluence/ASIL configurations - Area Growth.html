Question: How much area overhead when enabled Resiliency/Duplication.

Ans: Memory area almost looks same in ASIL A/B and smaller in NO_ASIL. So, the main difference is in logic.

Comparing NO_ASIL vs ASIL-B

               cell count difference :  7144350 – 4021852 = 3122498

                     % difference :  3122498 / 4021852 = 0.776  

 

               Total design area difference :  2295030 – 1863927 = 431103

                       % difference : 431103 / 1863927 = 0.23

                                                             

  • Cell area overhead due to Resiliency = ~78%

  • Overall area overhead due to Resiliency = ~23%
