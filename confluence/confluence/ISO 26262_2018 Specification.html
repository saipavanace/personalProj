This is the final FDIS prior to publication of the ISO 26262:2018. It is very close the published version and can be used in lieu of it. The published version costs lots of Swiss francs...

Also, you can see the changes in the ISO 26262:2018 Part 1 vocabulary vs. the 2011 definitions by going to https://www.iso.org/obp/ui/#iso:std:iso:26262:-1:ed-2:v1:en and clicking on "Redlines ▼" at the top.
