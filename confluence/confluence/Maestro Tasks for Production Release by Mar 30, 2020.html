12ADM signal coalescing: mostly for the GUI performance2Ken LawEnd of December

IN DISCUSSION. TCL-level hooks for command-level signals in discussion.

Lowest-hanging fruit: disable signals (at least to the GUI) when entering a TCL command. (If everything implemented in libaccess, row 3, this can be an automatic behaviour of AdmRequest). Re-enable them on command completion. Another simple performance improvement is to send out a single notification instead of the whole queue of frozen signals in between.

More complex: implement general signal coalescing logic, and optimize the GUI to narrow down the list of signals it cares about. More powerful but requires lots more profiling and is likely to be a multiple-releases project.
