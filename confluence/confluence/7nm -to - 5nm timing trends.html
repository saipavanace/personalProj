My analysis:

  1. Timing improved in 5nm than 7nm. See table1 & 2 below based on single corners.

    1. Cells are faster in 5nm.

    2. DCNXT has better timing optimization engine.

    3. However, RC delays are more dominant.

  2. Multi Corner synthesis is better optimized compared to single corner optimization. See Table 3.

  3. Comparing DCNXT vs RTLA 5nm Multi corner results, RTLA timing has almost clean. See Table 4.

    1. however, RTLA paths have high Logic levels than DCNXT synthesis – this could be a potential problem to converge the timing in physical implementation.
