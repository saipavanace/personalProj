100% Coverage Closure Review

Line coverage holes are mostly in multi port fifo and basically related to doing multiple push for all level of occupation. When the fifos become large it is fairly hard to cover and creates a significant numbers of miss (in the c_write buffer and in the ccp).

Some config with small skid buffer also strugle to fill some buffers downstream due to some known restriction in the tb. Those cases are well covered in other configs.

Config7 would benefit extra work to ahev more eviction and maintenance operation on the tags.

Large combiantorial logic cones were typically removed for timeout logic (coverage would benefit to have it tested a bit more extensively) and some flm for hte responses due to design having id much larger than the siae of teh skid bufferes.

Overall coverage seemed better than 3.6 and had minor changes in rtl. The improvement is mostly due to teh removal of unused code.
