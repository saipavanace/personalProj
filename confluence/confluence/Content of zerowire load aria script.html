cprInterfaceDirs: `${process.env.REPO_PATH}/assets/interface`,
techNode: 'tsmc7',
topoMode: false,
incrementalOpt: false,

});

It is important that the rtlWrapperDir and hardMacroDbs path matches the memories folder that you copied at the step before

The checkOnly parameter allows to stop after reading the design.
